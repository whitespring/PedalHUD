# Code of Conduct

PedalHUD aims to be a respectful, constructive project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear and respectful in issues, pull requests, and reviews.
- Assume good intent and focus on the work, not the person.
- Accept feedback and disagreement without hostility.
- Keep discussion relevant to the project and useful to other contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, threats, or intimidation
- Personal attacks or discriminatory language
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith participation
- Publishing someone else's private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or moderate content that violates this code of conduct and may restrict participation when necessary.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the project maintainer rather than escalating in public.
